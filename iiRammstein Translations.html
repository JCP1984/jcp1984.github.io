What is Rammstein?


Rammstein is a "Deutsche Neue Härte" Band that made their debut album in 1995. Since then, their lineup, which consists of Till Lindemann, the lead singer and song writer; Richard Kruspe, the lead guitarist; Paul Landers, the rhythm guitarist; Oliver Riedleba, the bassist; Christoph Schneider, the sole drummer; and Christian "Flake" Lorenz, the sole keyboardist, has not changed in their 30ish years of being a band.

They currently have released 8 albums, each with 11 songs within them, although the re-released albums have some extra songs added.
